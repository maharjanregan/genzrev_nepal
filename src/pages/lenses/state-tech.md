## What the evidence indicates

- Nepal implemented platform restrictions including TikTok (2023) and broader restriction episodes (2025 reporting).
- Measurements indicate network-level interference consistent with blocking (OONI).
- Policy direction includes proposed/introduced social media regulation with broad content control provisions and civil society concerns.
