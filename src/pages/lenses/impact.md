## What the evidence indicates

- Platform bans can have measurable impacts on telecom revenue (reduced bandwidth consumption).
- TikTok use is concentrated among young users, so disruptions concentrate costs on Gen Z creators and micro-business funnels.
- Network-level interference demonstrates policy-to-infrastructure translation.
