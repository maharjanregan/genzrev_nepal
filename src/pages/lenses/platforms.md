## What the evidence indicates

- TikTok appears as the primary short-form virality engine in major reporting.
- Instagram/Facebook function as “evidence galleries” and repost channels.
- Discord appears as a high-salience coordination channel in some reporting.
- Messaging apps (e.g., Viber) and VPNs appear in ban-evasion narratives.
