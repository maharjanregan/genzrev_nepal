## What the evidence indicates

- Documentation describes protest restrictions and use of force.
- Documentation describes reprisals for online criticism (including arrests under the Electronic Transactions Act).
- Press freedom/digital rights groups warn proposed social media regulation could enable surveillance and content removal.
